Restaurant Menu App

Challenge Statement

The challenge was to design a dynamic and user-friendly digital menu system for a restaurant. The main problems included:

  • Providing real-time menu updates** without needing a full website refresh.
  • Ensuring seamless navigation** for customers on both mobile and desktop.
  • Allowing restaurant staff to update menu items quickly via an admin panel.
  • Optimizing performance** to load images and descriptions efficiently.

Through a combination of React.js, Firebase, and a REST API, I developed a scalable solution that enhances both customer experience and restaurant management.
